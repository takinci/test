# Item 41 — Implications for practice, including intended use and/or clinical role

### Describe the implications for practice, including the intended use and possible clinical role of the AI model. Describe the key impact the work may have on the field, including changes from current practice. Envision the next steps that one might take to build upon the results. Discuss any issues that would impede successful translation of the model into practice.

Explanation and Elaboration: It is essential to clearly define the model’s intended use to ensure a clear understanding of its role. Describe how the model may improve clinical practice, as well as potential unintended consequences, risks, new burdens imposed by working with and supervising the AI and new or additional requirements for successful deployment. If there are unsolved problems that stand as barriers to clinical implementation, identify these as areas where additional research is needed.
