About EcoRad

EcoRad is a sustainability intelligence platform for radiology departments, AI governance teams and healthcare organizations.

Core Features

  • Radiology energy and carbon tracking
  • AI sustainability analytics
  • Cloud and PACS infrastructure footprint estimation
  • Scenario comparison and intervention analysis
  • Exportable ESG-style reports

Deployment

Supports GitHub Pages, Docker, FastAPI backend deployment and SQLite local setup.
